About Us

Our experience in the industry spans more than 10 years serving Malaysians residents & businesses. Mogan Raja founded the agency with an emphasis on personal and business services. We continue to offer superior service to every client, finding the best coverage at a competitive price to meet every client’s personal and business insurance needs. Our philosophy is at all times to treat everyone with the utmost respect, integrity, committed, transparency, ethical practises and empathy. Our aim is not simply to sell insurance, but rather to help clients better understand their insurance coverage needs. We are always happy to answer any insurance questions clients may have. Our services available when you need us. We are always available to answer your questions, process changes, or promptly handle claims. We are only a phone call away, even after hours. Our dedication to client service allow you to reach us 24 hours-a-day.


Vision
