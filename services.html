We offer services as following

PRESERVING YOUR WEALTH
Temporary & Permanent Lost of Income Benefits

Life Insurance – Long Term/Short Term Payment & Coverage

Life Insurance Pays your family or another beneficiary in the event of your death. If anyone relies on your income for their financial wellbeing (i.e. a spouse, child, parents, etc), you should consider life insurance. Personal Life gives you peace of mind knowing that your loved ones will be protected against financial catastrophe if you no longer here to do so.


Disability Insurance

Disability Insurance gives protection for your income should you become disabled and unable to work due to an illness or injury. Your household expenses don’t stop when you are injured or ill. Disability coverage will allow you to continue payments for your mortgage, autos, utilities, and everyday living cost.


Critical Illness Insurance – Cover Early Stage & Advance Stage

Critical Illness insurance is the plan that protects your income in event of a major illness diagnosis. Major illness may prevent you from continue your work for certain period of time. Your household expenses don’t stop when you are ill. Critical Illness coverage will allow you to continue payments for your mortgage, autos, utilities, and everyday living cost.


Inpatient & Outpatient Medical Expenses

Medical & Health Insurance

Medical & Health Insurance policy is generally designed to cover the cost of private medical treatment, such as the cost of hospitalization and healthcare services, if you are diagnosed with covered illnesses or had an accident.


Protecting Your Business Revenue & Business Continuation

Key Man Insurance

Key Man Insurance is a life insurance policy that a company purchases on the life of an owner, a top executive, or an individua; considered critical to the business. This policy designed to protect the life of business from losses in the event of the death of key person.


ACCUMULATE YOUR WEALTH
Systematic Saving For Retirement - Short Term & Mid Term Saving Plan

Systematic Saving Plan is an insurance plan helps you to add your savings at your complete convenience. You can start saving any fixed amount with protection for the saving amount in monthly basis, over a period of time.


Child Higher Education Fund

Child Higher Education Fund is an insurance plan that helps you create a corpus for your child’s future, over a period of time.
